STAKE HOLDERS
------------------
1.Admin
2.User
3.Service provider


FLOW OF OPERATION
-------------------------
1.open the app
2.Login/Register
3.check for nearby car wash service provider
4.displays the results of nearby service providers
5.Customer chooses the desired service provider
6.Application provides various options to select like service package etc
7.customers selects a request->(request)->application takes the request
8.contact with the selected service provider
9.service provider then picks up the car from location of customer
10.customer pays through the app
11.Application then make a payment to the service provider after everthing is done.
